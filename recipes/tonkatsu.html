Ingredients

  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Rao's Lasagna Sauce, or sauce of choice
  • Ground Beef
  • Mozzarella Cheese, shredded
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Salt, pepper, garlic powder and italian seasoning
  • Whatever else you want to add to your lasagna

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350Â°F
  2. While oven is preheating, add ground beef and seasonings to skillet and cook on medium heat until browned
  3. Boil pasta sheets
  4. Combine sauce and ground beef
  5. Take portion of pasta sheets and lay out in a baking dish
  6. Cover pasta with sauce and beef mixture, then top with cheeses
  7. Layer another portion of pasta sheets and repeat previous steps until pasta is used
  8. Top with a final layer of sauce and cheeses
  9. Bake in the oven covered for a total of 1 hour or until cheese is desired look
  10. Bake uncovered for 5-10 minutes
  11. Last but not least, Enjoy!
