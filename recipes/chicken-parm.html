Ingredients

  • Chicken Cutlets
  • Rao's Marinara
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Breadcrumbs and Egg Mixture
  • Spaghetti noodles, or desired noodle

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350Â°F
  2. Boil noodles
  3. Coat chicken in breadcrumb and egg mixture
  4. Bake chicken in oven for 15-20 minutes
