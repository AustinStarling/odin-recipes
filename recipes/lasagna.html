Ingredients

  • Tonkatsu
  • Sauce, or sauce of choice
  • White Rice
  • Green Onion
  • Anything else you'd like to add
  • Breadcrumbs and Egg mixture

Steps

  1. Coat meat in breadcrumb mixture
  2. Cook in oil until cooked thoroughly
  3. Measure out 2 cups of white rice and wash
  4. Place in rice cooker for 20 minutes, times vary depending on rice cooker used
  5. Place rice in a bowl to serve
  6. Top with sliced tonkatsu and sauce
  7. Enjoy!
